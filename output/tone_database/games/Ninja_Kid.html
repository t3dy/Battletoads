Creates smooth, flowing melodies. Common in RPG town themes, love themes, and any passage that needs a lyrical quality. The constant volume makes the melody feel like a voice singing rather than an instrument plucking. Final Fantasy and Dragon Quest use this extensively.

Synth tip: Set sustain to maximum. The NES achieves this by writing the same volume value every frame — in MIDI terms, CC11 stays constant throughout the note.

For Ninja Kid specifically: Set attack to 0ms, decay to 0ms, sustain to 100%. The sustained envelope means CC11 stays near-constant — let the automation drive the volume, dont add ADSR shaping.
